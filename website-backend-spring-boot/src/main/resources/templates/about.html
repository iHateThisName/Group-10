ABOUT US

Dedicated and helpful

XXS is your friendly neighbourhood hiking equipment supplier.
Whatever you need, we've got it. Our workers are specialized in
helping you find the right product in the best possible quality
for you, and the prices are set in a way that it'll be a profitable
investment for your hiking life

Location

XXS is located in beautiful and iconic Ålesund, at the north-west
coast in Norway. This gives us a particular first-hand experience
with fine nature and stormy weather, resulting in a good
understanding of our customers needs

Our products and services

We sell only premium equipment from brands such as Bergans,
North Face, Devold and Mammut. All our employees are hiking
enthusiasts and we have tested all we sell in the shop.
